Save Thy Turtles

Save Thy Turtles is an advocacy that seeks to protect sea turtles and their habitats from the threats of pollution, climate change, and human activities. By reducing plastic consumption, supporting sustainable fishing practices, participating in beach cleanups, and spreading awareness, individuals can contribute to the conservation of these ancient creatures and ensure their continued existence in our oceans. It's a critical cause that needs everyone's support to maintain a healthy marine ecosystem and secure a sustainable future for both turtles and humans.
